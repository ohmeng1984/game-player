About Us

Welcome to Game-Player, your number one source about the latest games in PC, Playstation, and Mobile. We're dedicated to giving you the very best reviews, with a focus on fun, excitement and uniqueness.

Founded in 2018 by Mel, Game-Player has come a long way from its beginnings in Tuitt Bootcamp. When Mel first started out, his passion for computer games drove him to study web developing, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online game review.

We hope you enjoy our game reviews as much as we enjoy sharing them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Mel, Web Admin.
